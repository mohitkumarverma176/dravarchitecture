Project Overview

A Residence Built Around Living

The Munirika Residence is a landmark residential project demonstrating Drav Architecture's expertise in blending modern design sensibilities with practical living requirements. The project encompasses architectural planning, landscape design, and interior coordination.

Our team worked closely with the client to deliver a design that maximises natural light, integrates green spaces, and maintains a seamless flow between indoor and outdoor environments — hallmarks of the Drav Architecture approach.

The residence features carefully considered spatial planning, premium material selection, and detailed landscape integration including native plantings and a functional outdoor entertaining area.
