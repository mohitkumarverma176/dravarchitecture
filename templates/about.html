Our Story

About Drav Architecture.

A multidisciplinary design firm delivering architecture, landscape, and interior solutions across India. We believe good design is purposeful, enduring, and deeply human.

Our Philosophy

Design That Serves People & Place.

At Drav Architecture, we believe that the built environment profoundly shapes human experience. Our work begins with rigorous site analysis and deep listening to our clients — understanding not just what they want to build, but how they want to live.

We are committed to contextually sensitive, climatically responsive design that balances innovation with practicality. Whether it's a private residence in Bihar or a large-scale landscape in Greater Noida, we bring the same standards of care and creativity to every project.

Mission

To create architecture and landscapes that enrich lives, strengthen communities, and respect the natural environment.

Vision

To be India's most trusted multidisciplinary design studio, known for integrity, innovation, and lasting impact.
